OUR SERVICES

Business Transfers & Strategies is committed to assisting business buyers. We are not business brokers, nor do we represent business sellers. We are advisors to buyers who seek to acquire a business with annual sales up to $50 million.

OUR PROCESS

Business Transfers & Strategies uses a proprietary process to assist buyers in finding, evaluating, financing and closing on a business that meets their exact specifications. We are experts in finding companies that are not publicly listed for sale and guiding our clients through the entire acquisition process. Having direct access to business sellers ensures that you won't have to outbid other buyers.
